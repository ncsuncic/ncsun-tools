Ethical Finance / NIOP

LDC Centre

A business starts in 2013. Health disruption. A restart in 2019. Lockdown. Pre-diagnosis. Across a decade of disruption and ambition, no joined-up conversation ever happened between the systems involved.

Your task at this station

Station 4 · Legal Integrity 10:50 – 11:10
Station 4 of 5

Legal Integrity

Two voices. A partner's four-year family court case, still ongoing. Running alongside it: a parallel 669-page bundle. A second hearing. No order — which was a win. Four years. No funded navigator. Two people, navigating simultaneously.

Your task at this station

Search for family law support, legal aid, and court navigation services. Where does the system expect a person to become their own barrister? What exists, and what doesn't?

Conversation prompts

Station 5 · Connect-EnGINE 10:50 – 11:10
Station 5 of 5

Connect-EnGINE

2022–2024. Two people in one household navigating welfare and employment systems at the same time. The two systems were completely invisible to each other. The household stopped turning up — to both.

Your task at this station

Search for welfare support and employment services simultaneously. Map what each would have known about the other. Is there any point where the two systems would have communicated — or would they have remained invisible to each other?
