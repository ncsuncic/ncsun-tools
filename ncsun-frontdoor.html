NCSUN · The Front Door

Your voice matters here.

NCSUN is a community organisation based in Norwich. We connect people to the right support and make sure what communities know reaches the people who make decisions.

Everyone who contributes their experience is treated with dignity and respect.

This page lets you share your experience of public services in your own words.

You do not need to give your name.

You do not need to create an account.

We will transcribe what you say. You will be able to read it back before you send anything.

Your experience helps us identify patterns and show decision-makers what needs to change.

What happens to your words?

The diagram below shows the two paths your submission can take. You will choose which path at the end.

Arm 1 — Anonymous
You record your experience
Your transcript is reviewed by a caseworker within 48 hours
Any identifying details are removed
Your experience joins a pattern database — no names, no identifiers
Patterns are shared with decision-makers to drive system change
Arm 2 — Stay in touch
You record your experience
You leave your name and contact details
A caseworker contacts you within 7 days
Your experience is reviewed, anonymised, and added to the pattern database
Patterns are shared with decision-makers to drive system change

We check every submission for anything that could identify you before it is stored.

Your words are used to find patterns — not to identify you as an individual.

Nobody outside NCSUN receives anything that could identify you.

If you choose to leave your contact details, we aim to be in touch within 7 days.

You can ask us to remove your submission within 48 hours, if you left contact details and can tell us roughly when you submitted.

There are two ways to send your experience.

Both are equally valid. Choose whichever feels right for you.

Option A

Send anonymously

Your transcript is reviewed and added to the pattern database. No name. No contact details.

Option B

Stay in touch

Leave your details and a caseworker will contact you within 7 days. You receive a reference number.

Your transcript will be reviewed by a member of our team.
We will remove anything that could identify you before it is stored.
Your words will be used alongside others to identify patterns in how services are working.
No information that could identify you will ever be kept or shared.

If you would like us to follow up with you, leave your details below.

This is completely optional.

We aim to be in touch within 7 days.

You will receive a reference number so you can follow up with us if you need to.

Changed your mind?

If you left your contact details and submitted within the last 48 hours, you can ask us to remove your submission entirely.

Contact us at connection@ncsun.org and tell us roughly when you submitted. We will locate and delete it within 48 hours of your request.
